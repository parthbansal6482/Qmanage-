Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by selecting your preferred cafe from the navigation menu and browsing their menu items. Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout.

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 9:00 AM to 11:00 PM, seven days a week. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and order volume.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes! All our partner cafes offer a variety of vegetarian options. You can filter menu items by dietary preferences when browsing.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard.
